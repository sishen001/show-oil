Company Profile

Established in 2016, Rayson International Limited is a leading international energy trading firm headquartered in Hong Kong, China. With a footprint spanning across Asia and Africa, we are deeply engaged in the trading of crude oil, natural gas, sulfur and sulfur-related products.

Our annual trading volume exceeds 1 million metric tons, serving numerous clients in industries such as power generation, chemicals, and agriculture, playing a crucial role in the global energy supply chain.

Core Business Segments

We specialize in diversified trading in the energy sector, providing high-quality energy products and services to global clients

Crude Oil and Derivatives Trading

Middle East crude, Central Asian crude, and various refined oil products like gasoline, diesel, and jet fuel.

Procurement Sources: Middle East, Central Asia, and West Africa

Natural Gas Business

Sulfur & Derivatives

Professional energy transportation and storage solutions for efficient supply chains.
